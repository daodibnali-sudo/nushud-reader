Frequently Asked Questions

Is Arabic Word By Word really free?

Yes. Reading, translating, and saving words to My Cards are all completely free, with no account, login, or payment required.

What file types can I upload?

Plain text files (.txt), PDFs, Word documents (.docx), and images (photos or scans of a page). Scanned or photographed pages are automatically converted to text before translation.

Does it work with Arabic text that has harakat (diacritics)?

Yes, both typed text and text recognized from scanned images or photos can include harakat. Recognition accuracy on harakat depends on image quality — clean, high-resolution scans work best.

Which languages can I translate Arabic into?

Over 130 languages, covering every language Google Translate supports, not just English.

Can I translate a whole sentence instead of one word at a time?

Yes. Select or highlight any run of text with your mouse or finger and it will be translated as a full sentence, in addition to clicking individual words for their dictionary meaning.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There is no login or signup anywhere on the site. Saved words are stored locally in your own browser.

Where are my saved cards stored?

In your browser's local storage, on your own device. They are not uploaded to any account, so they won't follow you to a different browser or device, and clearing your browser data will remove them.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes, the site is fully responsive, and on phones you can tap "Full display read" to expand the reading area to fill your entire screen.
