How It Works

Reading Arabic with word-by-word translation takes four steps. No account, no setup, nothing to install.

1. Upload your text

Drag and drop a file onto the upload box, or click it to choose one from your device. Supported formats: plain text (.txt), PDF, Word documents (.docx), and images (photos or scans of a page). If the file is a scanned page or a photo, the text is automatically recognized (including harakat/ diacritics) before anything else happens.

2. Pick your translation language

Choose the language you want meanings shown in from the language selector — over 130 languages are supported, not just English.

3. Read and click

Your text appears fully formatted and every word is clickable. Tap any word to see its meaning appear instantly in the side panel (or as a popup on phones). Words are translated on demand as you read, so there's no long wait before you can start.

4. Select a sentence for full translation

Want more than one word at a time? Highlight/select a whole sentence or phrase with your mouse or finger, and it's translated as a complete sentence instead of word-by-word — useful for understanding grammar and context, not just vocabulary.

5. Save words you're learning

Found a word worth remembering? Click "Save card" in the word panel to add it to My Cards, saved locally in your browser for later review — no account required.

Bonus: full-screen reading on phone

On mobile, tap "Full display read" above the text to expand the reading area to fill your whole screen, hiding menus and controls until you tap "Exit full display."
